About

Built Different.
Filmed Raw.

Rg Ng Sur is a Filipino social media creator who turned a simple life into something the internet can't look away from — chainsaw-powered motorcycles, impossible DIY builds, and content that hits different.

From viral showdowns like Chainsaw vs Bajaj to 24-engine madness, every project is documented with the same grit he brings to the garage. What started as "mga ganap sa simpleng buhay" — the events of an ordinary life — became a global audience rooting for the underdog builder.

"Mga ganap sa simpleng buhay na meron ako. Minsan nangarap na maging kasing galing nyo rin." — Rg Ng Sur
