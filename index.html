About

Built Different.
Filmed Raw.

Rg Ng Sur is a Filipino creator, builder, and recording artist who turned a simple life into something the internet can't look away from — chainsaw-powered motorcycles, impossible DIY builds, and content filmed with raw, unfiltered energy.

From viral showdowns like Chainsaw vs Bajaj to 24-engine madness, every project is documented with the same grit he brings to the garage. Now stepping into music with Camera Rolling on Spoken Devotion Music, Rg Ng Sur brings that same hustle from the workshop to the studio.

"Mga ganap sa simpleng buhay na meron ako. Minsan nangarap na maging kasing galing nyo rin." — Rg Ng Sur
